About Us


Algebuckina Design is a small design and manufacturing company in Western Sydney Australia. It is owned and run by Bradley Turner, an Industrial Design graduate who is currently working a 2nd job in packaging design. At Algebuckina Design, our aim is to make common household electronic peripherals more open and environmentally friendly and reduce the amount of plastic used in their construction.

What we are working on

We are currently in the process of developing 2 products. The 1st is a wood keyboard that aims to reduce the amount of plastics used in modern keyboards, while maintaining a premium typing feel. And the other is an open source car head unit to keep older cars up to date on technology and reduce the need to upgrade, and to remove the lock in ecosystem cars and aftermarket head units currently employ.

What we offer

We also offer 2 services. Firstly we are equipped to make custom VR environments for customers. Including custom 3D Models of products and the ability to recreate interior spaces. Secondly we offer a simple custom website just like this one. It is based on Nodebb, which is a free open source forum service, and we utilise plug-ins to create a custom website on top of it.

If you're interested in any of these products or services, feel free to contact us.

Meet the team


Bradley Turner

Founder, CEO and Lead Designer

Hey, I'm Bradley Turner, and I am currently a packaging designer at abbe. In my spare time, I am the lead designer of both the head unit and keyboard, designing, developing and producing them. I will also be heading our services team once our products are launched.

With how the design industry is evolving, it felt like it was driving me to start my own business. Most design jobs are very repetitive, so I was looking for something where I could branch out and change things up without job hopping. Algebuckina Design is what I created, and I want to use it as an outlet for my creativity and brain children. You can check out what I, and my team, are working on here.
