Overview

The Wood Mechanical Keyboard v0.1 delivers the same mechanical keyswitch feel, but with a lower ecological footprint than most other mechanical keyboards. All components (bar key switches) are made in house or sourced locally, with the case, backplate and keycaps being made out of sustainable (wood type) from (source location).

The Keyboard can be purchased assembled, unnassembled or a barebones kit that lets you bring your own keyswitchs and keycaps. We also have the option of custom kits with more personable touches, please contact us to see what we can do for you.
