Service Information

Overview

Our website service offers the ability to host your own forum and webpages locally instead of paying for a hosting service. Our service is based on the NodeBB platform, which is a powerful, mobile ready and easy to use forum and website manager that we have years of experience working with at Algebuckina Design. We setup a Virtualbox VM for you to run on your own system, and for additional fees, maintain, support and develop your website. If you're interested in what NodeBB can offer your company or venture, see what it can do on their website.

We would like to stress that the website is yours once we send you the VM. No additional costs will incur unless you require us to make changes, provide support or add new features. NodeBB offers an ongoing fee for them to run your website for you, we want to put that control back in your hands.
