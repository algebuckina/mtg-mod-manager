Products

Wood Keyboard

Our flagship product. Developed over 2 years, the wood keyboard is a renewable and sustainable take on the mechanical keyboard. For those who want to stand out, while clicking heads or in the workplace, and still have the satisfaction of a mechanical keyswitch, this keyboard is for you.

Open Source Head Unit

Current car head units suck! The industry has stagnated with only a handfull of manufacturers only doing the bare minimum. We plan to put the technology back in the hands of the users with our kits based on a Raspberry Pi. Can include Android Auto!

Services

SWGEmu Mod Manager

Keeping track of mods in a video game is hard work, especially with a lot of mods come as loose files. Bradley Turner wanted to change that in Star Wars Galaxies, so we developed this mod manager for SWGEmu and Mod the Galaxy.

Virtual Reality Service

Virtual Reality might not be the most popular topic at the moment due to a certain social media company, but there is no doubt it has its place in both the gaming and marketing world. We offer a service to create a custom VR space/environment based in SteamVR, a free and easily accessible platform.
